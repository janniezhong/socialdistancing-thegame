About the game

Between Us is a game that raises awareness on how to maintain a healthy physical distance from those around you. The main objective is to live as long as possible without infecting or being infected from other people.

Instructions

The user can drag the main player left and right to dodge people who may potentially be infected by COVID-19. The main player has 3 masks, each serving as a life and for each time the player hits an obstacle with a mask, the player has half a mask detracted from their total lives. If they hit an obstacle without a mask, the player has a full mask detracted from their total number of masks. As the player survives longer and longer, the track will speed up, calculating the player's score based on how long they live. Once the player runs out of masks, the game ends.
